Did you know Purple Tea was not introduced for its amazing unique taste, but was instead cultivated for its medicinal purposes.

Purple tea is a rare tea variety grown in the Mountainous Kenya region, originated from the familiar tea leaf, Camellia Sinensis. It's unique red-purple coloured leaves offers...

a tea that is rich in anthocyanins. Cultivated in a cooler climate, at an altitude of between 4,500 and 7,500 feet, exposing the plants to much more intense sun rays allowing it to produce maximum levels of protective anthocyanins and polyphenols

About

We believe that all natures gifts should be shared uninterrupted providing teas with no colourants, no preservatives or additives.

At Jua Na Mlima, pronounced JOO-WU-NUM-LI-MA, our teas are carefully hand picked and sourced at high altitude astride the equator in the rich volcanic soil highlands of Kenya. This brings great reference to our company’s name in Swahili meaning the “Sun and the Mountain” keeping...

it true to its Kenya origin. We value good health and excited to offer our birth tea product “Purple Tea” which has been extensively scientifically proven to enhance your life.
